2026 MISSION BRIEFING: THE ACTIVATION

OBJECTIVE: Identify emerging toxicology threats and implement 2026 operational shifts.

🏛️ The Strategic Context

Success in the 2026 landscape is built on three Strategic Pillars. These foundational concepts—Vigilance, Response, and Connection—support our entire public health framework. Without all three, the system lacks the stability to function.

🎮 How to Play

  • Search the Rooms: Navigate through three distinct environments: The Lab, The Hub, and The Stability Center.
  • Extract the Pillars: Solve the puzzle in each room to reveal a Master Clue Word (Strategic Pillar).
  • The Final Decryption: You must record all three Pillars; they are required to unlock the Command Terminal in the final room.

👥 Assigned Roles

Before beginning, designate these roles within your group:

The Navigator Shares their screen and "drives" the mission, exploring the rooms and clicking potential clues. The Cluemaster Maintains the Pillar Log. They must write down each Master Clue Word as it is revealed. If the Cluemaster loses a Pillar, the team cannot solve the final room.
