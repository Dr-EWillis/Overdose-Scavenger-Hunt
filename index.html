MISSION BRIEFING: THE ACTIVATION

OBJECTIVE: Identify emerging threats and trends. Implement 2026 operational shifts.

🏛️ The Strategic Context

The overdose landscape has shifted. We are no longer just fighting opioids. We are fighting a complex polysubstance crisis involving high-potentcy synthetic sedatives and undetected analogues. Success in the 2026 landscape is built on three Strategic Pillars. These are the foundational concepts that support our public health framework. Without all three, the system lacks the stability to function.

🎮 How to Play

  • Search the Rooms: You will enter three distinct environments (The Lab, The Hub, and The Operations Center).
  • Extract the Pillars: Search for clues and solve the puzzle in each room (one word answers) to reveal a Master Clue Word (Strategic Pillar).
  • The Final Decryption: You will need all three Pillars in the final room to unlock the Master Encryption and activate the Protocol.

👥 Assigned Roles

Designate these roles within your group before beginning:

The Navigator Shares their screen and "drives" the mission, exploring the rooms and clicking clues.

The Cluemaster Maintains the Pillar Log. They must write down each Master Clue Word as it is revealed. If the Cluemaster loses a Pillar, the mission will fail.
